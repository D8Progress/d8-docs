## Create Your Account

1. Go to [app.d8progress.com](https://app.d8progress.com) (or your self-hosted URL)
2. Click **Sign Up**
3. Enter your email and create a password
4. You're in!

## Create Your First Goal

A **Goal** is a big-picture outcome you're working toward. Examples:

- "Get healthier"
- "Learn Spanish"
- "Ship my side project"

To create one:

1. Click **Goals** in the sidebar
2. Click **+ New Goal**
3. Give it a name and optionally pick a category
4. Save it

## Add an Objective

**Objectives** break goals into manageable chunks. For a simple goal, you might have just one objective. For complex goals, you might have several.

## Add Tasks

**Tasks** are the actual things you do. They're the checkboxes.

1. Open an objective
2. Click **+ Add Task**
3. Type what you need to do
4. Assign it to a week (optional but recommended)

## Check the Weekly View

The **Weekly View** is your command center. It shows everything you planned to do this week, grouped by category and goal.

1. Click **Weekly** in the sidebar
2. See your tasks for this week

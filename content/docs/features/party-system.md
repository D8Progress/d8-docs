> **Note:** The Party System is available starting in version 0.5.

The **Party System** is Deliber8 Progress's most playful feature. Each goal becomes a character that you level up by working on it.

## The Concept

In RPGs, you have a party of characters, each with their own class, abilities, and progression. Deliber8 Progress applies this to your goals:

- **Goal** â†’ **Character**
- **Completing tasks** â†’ **XP for that character**
- **Character levels up** â†’ **Equip gear from the shop**

## Creating Characters

When you create a goal, you can optionally enable Party Mode for it:

1. Create or edit a goal
2. Toggle "Enable Party Character"
3. Choose:
   - **Name** (can differ from goal name)
   - **Class** (Wizard, Warrior, Druid, etc.)

Your character starts at level 1 and gains XP whenever you complete tasks under that goal.

1. Browse items and filter by category or rarity
2. Toggle "Hide owned items" to see what you don't have
3. Click an item to see details
4. Click **Purchase** if you have enough gold
5. Item appears in your Inventory

## Inventory

Access your **Inventory** (ğŸ’ in the navbar) to see purchased items:

- View all items you own, organized by type
- See which items are equipped and on which character
- **Equip** items to characters from here
- Some items have class restrictions shown

### Equipping Items

1. Find the item in your inventory
2. Click **Equip**
3. Choose which character to equip it on
4. The item appears on that character's gear slot

Each character has three gear slots: Head, Body, and Accessory.
